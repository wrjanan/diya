Intro

Good day, allow me to introduce this e-gallery, as it serves various purposes.

Firstly, this gallery, like most, are for your eyes to feast upon and enjoy. These images have been captured in a timeless fashion by my guidance and equipment. For your viewing pleasure, and if you require assistance in acquiring rights for the images, I am here to assist you.

Secondly, I want to put my pieces out there and look forward to all forms of feedback. Granted, if you like my work, I am, as an artist, more than pleased; however, constructive criticism is also welcome, as with all art.

Lastly, it is a great honour to put our work up here for all to see. It drives me to perfect my craft and I wish to be the very best.

Do check out my work.

Do not forget to voice any queries and comments here.



About

Photography has been a passion since 12 years of age. Am known for quality work in high fashion, though commercial jobs are much welcomed too.

At Diya M. Studios, we strive to be reliable and consistent. As such, once a date and time is fixed for a shoot, it is fixed and we will be there. Please understand that a lot of effort is put into collaborative shoots and we respect the time that others are willing to invest in them. Henceforth, we look forward to a long term working relationship, as a professional and reliable photography studio for your quality photography needs.

Find out how to get in touch with us here.
